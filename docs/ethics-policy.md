# Ethics Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the ethical boundaries and expectations for building, testing, and using this project. It is intended to protect peopleâ€™s safety, privacy, and legal rights, and to prevent misuse.

## Prohibited Uses
The project must not be used to:
- Conduct non-consensual tracking of individuals.
- Enable stalking, harassment, intimidation, or similar harms.
- Support law enforcement or government use without valid, legally required warrants or equivalent judicial authorization.

## Consent and Legal Compliance
Use of this project requires:
- Explicit, informed user consent from all affected individuals where applicable.
- Compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations in the jurisdictions involved.
- Respect for privacy, data minimization, and purpose limitation.

## Development and Testing
Development and testing are permitted only when all of the following conditions are met:
- The activity is conducted in controlled environments with clear scope and safeguards.
- Test data is synthetic or fully authorized by the data subjects.
- The work does not violate the prohibited uses above.
- Any deployment is limited to ethical, lawful use cases with documented consent.

## Statement of Intent
This project is intended solely for ethical, lawful, and consensual applications. Any use or contribution must align with this policy, and violations may result in removal of access or other corrective actions.
